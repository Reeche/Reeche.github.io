Ruiqi He

ruiqi.he@outlook.com

Advancing insights into human metacognitive learning behaviour.

My research lies in the intersection between machine learning and cognitive science, where I design and conduct online experiments and apply machine learning frameworks to model the underlying learning mechanism to draw inference on the human metacognitive learning behaviour.

My research interest include cognitive modelling, multi-agent systems and game theory.

Apart from doing research, I enjoy most of my time travelling and cooking.
