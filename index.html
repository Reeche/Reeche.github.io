About Me

I am a PhD reseacher at Dr. Falk Lieder's Rationality Enhancement Group at Max Planck Institute - Intelligent Systems in Tübingen. My research topic aim to examine and model human metacognitive learning in the context of planning using reinforcement learning models. That is, how do people learn how to plan so efficiently despite limited cognitive resources? My research interests include: computational modelling, reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, game theory, hierarchical planning.

Next to my academic work, I work part-time as project manager at E. Breuninger GmbH in Stuttgart, a leading fashion and lifestyle company, where I focus on being the bridge between the business and implementation teams to deliver high-impact data science projects.
